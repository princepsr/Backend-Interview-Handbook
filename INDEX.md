## How to Use This Index

This index has two complementary sections:

1. **Alphabetical Topic Index** — find any concept instantly. Each entry shows which chapter(s) cover it, so you can jump directly to the right file. Where a topic spans multiple chapters, all references are listed.

2. **Chapter-by-Chapter Breakdown** — if you are studying a chapter sequentially, see every topic it covers at a glance. Revision chapters (Volume 6) intentionally overlap with earlier chapters and are listed separately.

**Chapter number abbreviations used throughout:**
Ch1–Ch6 = Volume 1 (Core Java) | Ch7–Ch8 = Volume 2 (Spring) | Ch9–Ch13 = Volume 3 (Backend Systems) | Ch14–Ch18 = Volume 4 (Databases) | Ch19–Ch22 = Volume 5 (System Design & LLD) | Ch23–Ch27 = Volume 6 (Revision Pack)
